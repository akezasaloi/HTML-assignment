Life at AkiraChix during orientation week!

During my first weeek at AkiraChix campus, I had so much fun learning various cultures from all around the eastern part of Africa.
In addition to socialising with fellow collegues from different countries, we had some not so fun activities planned for us as well as some challenging rules to follow such as:

  • Daily cleaning of our rooms, classes and the common area
  • Weekly general cleaning of our rooms and the common area
  • Leaving phones in our rooms for all class hours

Regardless of all that though, AkiraChix had a lot of enjoyable things to offer for example:

  1. A tour of the campus by the alumni
  2. Four daily delicious and nutritious meals
  3. Hot showers in the mornings!
  4. A great First Aid & Safety Awareness training session
  5. Role play educative and funny presentation

In sum, all AkiraChix staff and alumni have been nothing but welcoming and kind, Infact they even provided me a room to pray and accomodated me while I was fasting!
It might have been hard to adjust to the quick schedule but all things considered I had a great experience.
